About Us

ChasetheEarth was founded by a university student who got tired of hearing about the world's problems without seeing enough action.

We believe that everyone has the power to make a difference. So, to make our difference, we've decided that with every purchase, we will contribute a portion of the profits to charity.

What sets us apart from the rest? Well, you get to choose where it goes.
But we're not just about charity; we're also about quality. Our clothes are built to last. We use top-notch materials and pay attention to every detail, so you know you're getting the best of the best. Plus, we believe in transparency. That's why we'll let you in on all the specifications of our clothing, from the type of fabric we use to every stitch and seam.

Join us on our journey to redefine fashion—one that embraces both beauty and purpose. Together, let's chase the change we wish to see in the world.
Thank you for supporting our vision.

With gratitude,
Roman Pinto

Founder, ChasetheEarth
